Jaheim S. [meteor rush ~ 1.0.3]

Instructions

Use WASD or Left/Right Arrow Keys to move your spaceship.
Dodge falling meteors. Survive as long as you can to increase your score.
The game ends if you hit an meteor. Your final score will be displayed.
